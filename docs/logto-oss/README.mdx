# Logto OSS

Logto offers both open-source (OSS) and [cloud](/logto-cloud) versions. This section will focus on the OSS solution: <Url type="inline" href="https://github.com/logto-io/logto">github.com/logto-io/logto</Url>

Logto OSS is a free-to-use identity solution that provides developers with full customization capabilities and complete control over their data. Logto OSS offers the flexibility to adapt the codebase to meet specific needs, it’s a perfect fit for those who prioritize data sovereignty and want a tailored identity provider solution that can grow with their application.

## Feature supported by Logto OSS

Logto OSS supports most core capabilities of the Logto service and is regularly updated and maintained.

Some advanced features are currently exclusive to the Logto Cloud version, including [Logto Protected App](/integrate-logto/protected-app/), [free email service](/connectors/email-connectors/built-in-email-service), [bring your UI](/customization/bring-your-ui), [IdP-initiated SSO](/end-user-flows/enterprise-sso/idp-initiated-sso), and SAML IdP.
